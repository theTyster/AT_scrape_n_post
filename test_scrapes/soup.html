The following information is from the Adventure Time comic book series . It may not be part of the same canon or continuity as the animated series.

Volumes/Trade Paperbacks This page is for the Volumes and Trade Paperbacks of the different Adventure Time Comics. For reference, Trade Paperback, means a collection of Issues in one book, Volume means there are multiple Trade Paperbacks of that issue line. All "first covers" are softcover, unless noted. (die-cut, means the cover has a hole in it as part of the design) (Volumes and TPB books are considered graphic novels because of their length)

Trivia

  • According to Ted Anderson, Season 11 was cancelled by BOOM! due to low sales, making the Issue 6 the final issue of the series. The producing layout of the Issue 7, however, had gotten done before the decision was made. [2]
  • In an AMA held for the comics, it was revealed that 12 issues in total were planned for Season 11, made up by the first 4 issues as the first arc "Empress Marceline", two issues for " Humantown " arc, two single issues, and ended by a 4-issue season finale. [3] [4]
    • In Issue 7, the storyline would focus on Jermaine , who becomes a successful painter, with Jake attempting to be more bonded with his sibling. [3] [4] [5]
    • For Issue 8, the storyline would have been about Finn, who would feel out of purpose after the adventures he had. [3] [4] [6]
    • The season finale would have made a return of Dr. Gross , who plotted a genetic plague, and Samantha . [3] [4] [7]
  • Despite that Issue 7 to 12 were never released, Julie Benbassat designed variant covers for each of them. [8] [9] [10]
